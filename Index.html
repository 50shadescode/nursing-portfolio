About Me

RN MSN PhD

I specialize in providing individualized and comprehensive research and academic support designed for students navigating undergraduate and graduate-level nursing programs.

My approach involves a deep understanding of each student's unique academic needs, learning style, and career goals. This includes:

Research Guidance

Offering step-by-step assistance through the entire research process, from refining a topic and conducting literature reviews to data analysis and manuscript preparation.

Academic Mentorship

Providing personalized coaching on advanced study skills, evidence-based practice, academic writing, and time management to boost student success and retention.

Additionally, I facilitate a variety of flexible learning pathways that offer students adaptability in terms of pace, place, and mode of delivery. This ensures students—including those with full-time employment, family responsibilities, or non-traditional educational backgrounds—have equitable access to high-quality education.
